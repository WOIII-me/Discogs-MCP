# Axes and evidence

## Sonic

Rank likely sound quality from mastering credits, matrix/runout evidence, pressing plant,
format, reputable reissue signals, and community evidence. A sonic score is a heuristic;
it is not an audio measurement or proof that one copy will sound better.

## Collector

Rank edition desirability from provenance, scarcity, demand, format, label/pressing
signals, and community evidence. Do not equate price with historical importance or
guarantee future value.

## Value

Balance evidence quality against marketplace price and availability only when those
fields are present and permitted. Treat marketplace observations as dynamic snapshots,
not offers, quotes, or guarantees.

## Interpretation

- Prefer concrete signals over a score alone.
- Report evidence coverage and thin or conflicting evidence.
- Keep observed facts separate from derived verdicts.
- Preserve partial/truncation warnings; do not silently promote an incomplete survey.
- Compare on one axis at a time unless the user explicitly requests separate rankings.
